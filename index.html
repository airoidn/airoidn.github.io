The Problem
Osaka Indoor Air Quality · 2025

Osaka's air
is silently
hurting you.

Indoor air quality across Osaka registers up to 5× worse than outdoor levels. Modern sealed buildings trap VOCs, fine particulate matter (PM2.5), allergens, and chemical off-gassing — invisible threats that accumulate throughout the day.

With over 2.7 million residents spending up to 90% of their time indoors, chronic exposure has become a public health crisis quietly hidden behind closed doors — linked to asthma, cardiovascular disease, and measurably reduced cognitive performance.

Existing solutions demand permanent installation, constant electricity, and expensive upkeep. For renters, travelers, offices, and emergency scenarios, there was simply no answer. Until AIRO.

The Solution

Portable power.
Clean air.
Zero compromise.

AIRO pairs a medical-grade dual-layer filtration system with an integrated solar charging array and 48-hour battery — delivering uncompromising air purification anywhere on earth, forever off-grid.

One device.
Infinite clean air.

Weighing just 800g — the size of a large water bottle — AIRO delivers industrial-grade air purification without cords, without installation, and without a carbon footprint. Charge it once from the sun and it breathes for two full days.

  • H13 True HEPA + activated carbon dual-layer filter
  • Integrated 12W monocrystalline silicon solar array
  • 6,000 mAh LiFePO₄ battery — 48h on medium mode
  • Real-time PM2.5, VOC, CO₂ equiv., temp & humidity display
  • USB-C 45W fast-charge fallback for cloudy conditions
  • Covers up to 25 m² in under 30 minutes
  • Whisper-quiet at 24 dB — safe for sleep & focus work
  • IP54 rated · 80% recycled enclosure · 10-year build life

Every component inside AIRO was chosen for performance, longevity, and environmental responsibility. Nothing is accidental.

01 / POWER

Solar Array

Monocrystalline silicon panels with a 23.1% conversion efficiency — among the highest available in portable form. The integrated fold-flat design angles for optimal sun capture in any orientation.

  • Output 12W peak
  • Efficiency 23.1%
  • Material Monocrystalline Si
  • Charge time 4–6 h (full sun)
02 / FILTRATION

H13 True HEPA

Medical-grade H13 HEPA captures 99.97% of particles at 0.3 microns — including PM2.5, bacteria, mold spores, and viral aerosols. Tested to EN 1822 standard. Replaceable every 6 months.

  • Grade H13 EN 1822
  • Efficiency 99.97% @ 0.3 μm
  • Targets PM2.5, VOC, Allergens
  • Filter life ~6 months
03 / BATTERY

LiFePO₄ Battery

Lithium iron phosphate chemistry provides unmatched thermal stability, safety, and cycle life. 6,000 mAh delivers 48 hours on medium mode or 16 hours at full turbo output.

  • Capacity 6,000 mAh
  • Chemistry LiFePO₄
  • Cycle life2,000+ @ 80% DoD
  • Charge in USB-C 45W PD
04 / SENSORS

Air Quality Sensors

Dual-sensor array measures PM2.5, PM10, VOC index, CO₂ equivalent, temperature, and relative humidity. Updated every 2 seconds — displayed on the e-ink panel and synced to the AIRO companion app.

  • PM sensing Laser particle counter
  • VOC sensing MOx semiconductor
  • Update rate Every 2 seconds
  • Display E-ink, sunlight-readable
05 / HOUSING

Recycled Enclosure

Injection-molded from 80% post-consumer recycled ABS plastic. Rated IP54 for splash and dust resistance — engineered to survive a decade of daily use in homes, offices, and outdoor settings.

  • Material 80% rABS plastic
  • IP Rating IP54
  • Weight 800 g
  • Dimensions 85 × 85 × 220 mm
06 / CARBON FILTER

Activated Carbon

Coconut-shell derived activated carbon adsorbs VOCs, formaldehyde, benzene, odors, and nitrogen dioxide. Works in tandem with HEPA for comprehensive molecular-level air purification.

  • Source Coconut shell carbon
  • Targets VOC, NO₂, Formaldehyde
  • Surface area >1,000 m²/g
  • Filter life ~6 months
